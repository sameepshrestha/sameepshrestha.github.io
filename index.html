ROBOTICS - ABU ROBOCON

The Asia pacific Robot Contest features 20 nations from all around Asia with big names such as Japan, China, India and Hongong. For the competition, different universities from these Asian Nation comes to show off their technology and for sharing knowledge. Nepali students from Tibuvan University from pulchowk campus represent Nepal every year.

I participated in the competition for all my undergrad years from 2016-2020. For 2019 robocon we had two robots per the rules of the challenges. The robot has three actuators with two for linear motion and one for angular. The other robot for the competition was a carrier robot with holonomic drive to carry bigger objects and store them in a location and a physical messenger for the quadruped robot.
